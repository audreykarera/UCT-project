Welcome to Inspire Academy


Inspire Academy is the Maths, Science, Accounting and ICT tutorship programme of IFG Africa. The Academy was established in 2013 to assist the Eastern Cape government in improving the pass rates for the key higher learning institution gateway subjects. Inspire Academy now has The Academy supports learners from Grade 6 - 12. Inspire Academy now has 4 centres accross South Africa, namely, 2 in Bisho, 1 in King Williams Town and 1 in Soweto.
